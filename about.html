About MCCA Studio Design

MCCA Studio Design Company is an architecture design firm located in Gisozi, Kigali, Rwanda. Our team is dedicated to bringing innovative and sustainable architectural solutions to our clients. We specialize in creating spaces that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing, contributing to the growth and development of the Rwandan architecture landscape.

Our Mission

At MCCA Studio Design, our mission is to create innovative and sustainable architecture that enhances the environment and improves the quality of life. We aim to bring creative and forward-thinking design solutions to every project we take on, whether residential, commercial, or public spaces.

Our Vision

We envision being a leading architecture firm in Rwanda and beyond, known for our commitment to quality, sustainability, and design excellence. Our vision is to contribute to the development of Rwanda’s architectural landscape through unique and impactful designs.

Our Team

Our team is composed of talented professionals who are passionate about architecture and design. We work together to bring our clients' ideas to life, combining creativity, functionality, and technical expertise. We value collaboration, innovation, and attention to detail in every project.
